Description

Jawan is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film co-written and directed by Atlee (in his Hindi film debut). It is produced by Gauri Khan and Gaurav Verma under Red Chillies Entertainment. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan in a dual role as lookalike father and son alongside Nayanthara (in her Hindi film debut) and Vijay Sethupathi. In the film, Azad, the jailer of a woman's prison, is a vigilante who recruits inmates and commits acts that shed light on corruption in India.
